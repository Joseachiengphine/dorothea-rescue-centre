## üé® DESIGN STYLE GUIDELINES

### Layout Inspiration
- **Clean, modern, minimalist** (like ALTRUISM)
- **Bold, impactful** (like Smile Orphanage)
- **Split-screen compositions**
- **Large, powerful imagery**
- **Strong typography**
- **Plenty of white/negative space**

### Photography Style
- **High contrast** black and white portraits
- **Direct eye contact** for emotional connection
- **Professional, authentic** moments
- **Warm, natural lighting**
- **Soft focus backgrounds** (bokeh effect)
- **Emotional depth** and authenticity

### Color Usage
- **Jungle Green (#29AB87)**: Primary accent, buttons, highlights, nature elements
- **Yellow (#FFFF00)**: Energy, call-to-action buttons, highlights, warmth
- **Maroon (#4E1B1B)**: Strength, backgrounds, text, traditional elements
- **Black/White**: Powerful portraits, contrast, modern feel
- **Cream/Beige**: Warm backgrounds, balance
