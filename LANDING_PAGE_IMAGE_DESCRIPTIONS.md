## Image Style Guidelines

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Maroon (#4E1B1B or #5D100A)
- **Accent:** Gold/Yellow (#FFFF00 or #FDD20A)
- **Supporting:** Warm creams, soft beiges, gentle oranges
- **Avoid:** Harsh colors, cold tones, overly bright or saturated colors

### Photography Style:
- Warm, natural lighting
- Soft focus backgrounds (bokeh effect)
- Professional, high-quality images
- Emotional connection and authenticity
- Diverse representation
- Positive, hopeful expressions

### Mood & Tone:
- **Hopeful** - Conveying optimism and new beginnings
- **Compassionate** - Showing care and understanding
- **Safe** - Creating a sense of security and protection
- **Inspiring** - Motivating action and support
- **Professional** - Maintaining credibility and trust
- **Warm** - Using warm color tones and lighting

## Image Generation Tips

When generating these images using AI tools (like DALL-E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion), use these prompts as a base and add:

- **For AI Image Generators:**
  - "Professional photography"
  - "Warm lighting"
  - "Soft focus background"
  - "Emotional, authentic"
  - "High quality, 4K"
  - "Color palette: maroon, gold, cream"

- **Avoid:**
  - Overly posed or staged looks
  - Cold or clinical atmospheres
  - Harsh lighting
  - Negative or sad expressions
  - Cluttered backgrounds
